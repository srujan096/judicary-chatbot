About the Indian Judiciary Chatbot

The Indian Judiciary Chatbot is an AI-powered tool designed to assist citizens with legal information, court procedures, and resources within the Indian judicial system. Whether you need help understanding your rights, filing a case, or tracking a court matter, this chatbot provides guidance in a simple and accessible way.

Inspired by the principles of justice, equality, and accessibility enshrined in the Constitution of India, this chatbot aims to empower individuals with knowledge and connect them to legal support services. It is not a substitute for professional legal advice but a companion for navigating the system.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I file a case?

Visit your local court or use the e-Courts portal to file online. You'll need to submit required documents and pay applicable fees.

What is legal aid?

Legal aid provides free legal services to eligible individuals under the Legal Services Authorities Act, 1987.

How can I check my case status?

Use the e-Courts portal or the case status section below with your case number to track updates.
